About CornOrb

Understanding the technology behind our diagnostic system

Multi-Map Fusion

Analyzes 4 corneal topography maps simultaneously — Axial, Anterior Elevation, Posterior Elevation, and Pachymetry — through a 12-channel tensor for comprehensive corneal assessment.

Vision Transformer

Powered by ViT-B/16, which processes images as sequences of patches using self-attention, capturing long-range spatial dependencies for superior global pattern recognition of corneal deformation.

99.07% Accuracy

Our model achieved 99.07% validation accuracy through careful training with data augmentation, class-weighted loss, cosine annealing, and early stopping strategies.
